**Kraft Masonry** is a minimal Hugo theme for personal blogs.

This is the demo site. Replace this page with your own introduction — who you are, what the blog covers, and where to find you elsewhere.

The theme supports multiple post categories, a client-side category filter, a tag taxonomy with a clickable tag cloud, bento-style masonry cards on the homepage, and a light/dark mode toggle that respects your system preference.

No external dependencies. No build step beyond `hugo server`.
